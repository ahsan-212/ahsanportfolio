Where i Live

I live in Lahore, a vibrant and historic city that is often referred to as the cultural heart of Pakistan. Known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and bustling streets, Lahore is a city that beautifully blends tradition with modernity. From the majestic Badshahi Mosque and the iconic Lahore Fort to the lively food streets and colorful bazaars, there is always something to explore. The city is also home to lush green parks, prestigious educational institutions, and a thriving arts and culture scene. Living in Lahore means experiencing a unique mix of warmth, hospitality, and a deep sense of heritage that makes it truly special.
